ABOUT

Hey there! I'm Aaron, a music creator specializing in intense electronic and metal genres. Although I enjoy doing many other genres too.

I started making music back when I was studying at uni and now I make a living composing for video games and streaming services.

I also enjoy coding small programs from time to time as well as trying new software to make my music.

These are some random facts about me:

  • Loud music enjoyer.
  • Big Half-Life and Doom nerd.
